Perception of Yourself

Positive Sense of Self

Developing a positive sense of self comes with self esteem, stress management and confidence. Through these factors we further act upon our decisions or actions. When we have a positive self sense we tend to take healthy risks to push ourselves to the best of our abilities. Having confidence helps us to bounce back from setbacks and encourages us to have positive interactions with others. When we have a positive sense of self we are less likely to become victims of negative peer influence or experience stress and we also have opportunities with our academics and career aspirations.
